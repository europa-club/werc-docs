# Security Policy

## Overview

Euro Finance (WERC) is a transparent, non-custodial, decentralized digital asset.

We prioritize security, transparency, and verifiable on-chain behavior.

## Key Security Principles

- Smart contracts are open-source and publicly verifiable
- No hidden minting, blacklist, or trading restrictions
- No honeypot mechanics (buy/sell fully functional)
- Ownership is renounced or restricted where applicable
- All transactions are verifiable on-chain

## Networks
